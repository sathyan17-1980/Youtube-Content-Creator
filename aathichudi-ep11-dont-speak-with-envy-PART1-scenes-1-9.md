## STORY: "The Sky Garden Competition"

Mayuri the Peacock and a new competitor, Ravi the Parrot, participate in the annual Sky Garden Competition to find rare magical flowers. When Ravi succeeds first, Mayuri struggles with envy. Through adventure, self-discovery, and Guru's wisdom, Mayuri learns that celebrating others' success brings greater rewards than jealousy. The story demonstrates the harmful nature of envy and the beauty of genuine celebration through action-packed flying, puzzle-solving, and magical discoveries.
