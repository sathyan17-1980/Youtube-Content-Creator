## **SCENE 13: The Mirror Lake Truth** (26 seconds)

### Visual Description
Mirror Lake - a perfectly still body of water floating in the sky, its surface so calm it reflects not just physical appearance but the truth within one's heart. Mayuri, shaken by Guru's teaching, lands beside the lake alone. She cautiously approaches the water's edge and looks down. Her reflection doesn't show her physical feathers and colors - instead, it shows her HEART: currently wrapped tightly in shadow vines, each vine representing an envious thought or bitter word. **Emotional Beat:** Honest self-confrontation and active choice to release envy
**Camera:** Lake approach, reflection close-up, ripple effects, confession sequence, transformation
