# "அஃகஞ் சுருக்கேல்" (Akam Surukkel - Don't Be Stingy in Selling Food Grains)

**Episode Number:** 12
**Tamil:** அஃகஞ் சுருக்கேல்
**Meaning:** அஃகம் - தானியம், சுருக்கேல் - குறைக்காதே
**Paraphrase:** தானியங்களை குறைத்து விற்க வேண்டாம் (Do not be stingy in selling food grains)
**Moral:** உணவுப் பொருட்களில் நேர்மை முக்கியம் (Honesty in food is important)
**Lesson:** Fair measure in trade brings lasting prosperity; dishonesty leads to ruin

**Format:** 16:9 LANDSCAPE
**Total Scenes:** 18 (Part 1: Scenes 1-9)
**Animation Software:** OpenArt (will be split into 8-10s sub-clips in REVISED version)
**Language:** Bilingual (Tamil + English)

## STORY: "The Honest Merchant"

Mayuri the Peacock visits the legendary Cloud Grain Market where magical grains that feed all the sky villages are traded. She meets two merchants: Dharma the Dove (honest) and Kanjan the Crow (dishonest). When Kanjan's rigged scales are exposed by the Grain Guardian spirits, Mayuri witnesses the consequences of dishonest measure versus the rewards of fair trade. Through flying adventures, magical grain fields, ancient scale puzzles, and honesty tests, Mayuri learns that integrity in food trade is sacred and brings abundance to all.
